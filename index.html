Why Choose Us

The Cardiff Experts in Solid Wood Restoration

At Coedwig Creations, we believe every piece of wood has a story and a natural beauty waiting to be rediscovered. Founded by Ashley Coombes, we specialize in transforming worn, scratched, or dull kitchen worktops into stunning centerpieces.

Our meticulous process ensures a finish that isn't just beautiful, but durable enough for the busiest of kitchens. We use industry-leading tools and premium hardwax oils to ensure perfection.
